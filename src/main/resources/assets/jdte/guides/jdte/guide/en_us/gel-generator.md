# Gel Generator

The Gel Generator consumes input items to produce gel-related results, supporting fluid and energy capabilities.

## Variants

| Machine | Upgrade Slots | Energy Capacity | Fluid Capacity |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Advanced Gel Generator | 4 | 100,000 FE | 4,000 mB |
| Extended Gel Generator | 8 | 200,000 FE | 4,000 mB |

## Features

- Consumes fuel items to produce gel fluid
- Supports redstone control, Range Upgrade, and Filter Upgrade
- Supports Overclock, Underclock, and Capacity Upgrades
- Food items provide 2 uses
