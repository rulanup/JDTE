# Extended Advanced Machines

Extended advanced machines have 8 upgrade slots, with other functionality identical to the original T2 machines.

They can be obtained through crafting or by using <ItemImage id="jdte:extended_upgrade" scale="1" /> [Extended Upgrade](extended-upgrade.md) to directly convert T2 machines.
