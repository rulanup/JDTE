# Boss Essences

Boss Essences are unique items obtained by crushing bosses with the Bio Crusher.

## Essences

### Wither Essence

Obtained by crushing the Wither boss.

### Ender Dragon Essence

Obtained by crushing the Ender Dragon boss.

### Elder Guardian Essence

Obtained by crushing the Elder Guardian boss.

## Obtaining

To obtain Boss Essences:

1. Build a Bio Crusher (Advanced or Extended)
2. Place it near a boss mob
3. The Bio Crusher will kill the boss and generate the corresponding essence

Note: The Bio Crusher must be powerful enough to kill the boss. Consider using Sharpness upgrades to increase damage.

## Future Uses

Boss Essences are currently collectible items. Future updates may add crafting recipes and special abilities using these essences.
